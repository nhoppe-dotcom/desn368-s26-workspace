Milkshake From Above

Prep time 5 minutes Yeild 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cups choclete ice cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepermint extract
  • 1/4 cup Junior Mints plus more for garnish
  • Whipped cream for garnish

Decription

In a blender combine the ice cream, milk, peppermint extract, and Junior Mints.

Blend on high until smooth.

Divide into two glasses, garnish with additional mints and whipped cream and serve.
