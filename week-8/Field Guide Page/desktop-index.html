The Maze Environment

  • Structure: A enclosed, two-dimensional structure featuring connected passages and a central "ghost pen".
  • Hazards: Beware of the "tunnel" on the far left and right; while it allows for quick traversal, it often forces a confrontation with ghosts in tight quarters.
  • Strategy: Prioritize clearing the bottom half of the maze early, as it offers the fewest exit routes and highest threat density.

Survival Mechanics: Power Pellets

  • Effect: Consuming these large pellets induces panic in the spectral inhabitants, causing them to turn blue and reverse direction.
  • Tactics:Do not waste these limited resources; activate them only when trapped or to secure bonus points by consuming the vulnerable, fleeing spirits.
  • Warnings: Their effectiveness is temporary and significantly shortened in higher-level territories.

The Explorer: Pac-Man
  • Profile: An insatiable, yellow, disc-shaped entity that thrives on high-energy dot consumption.
  • Behavior: Moves in straight lines and is incapable of turning around instantly, requiring a junction to change direction.
  • Notes: If cornered, the explorer must rely on sheer speed to maneuver out of immediate danger, as contact with hostile entities results in instant loss of vitality.

The Hostile Specters (Ghosts):
  • Three are 4 ghost they are Blinky (Red):The "Shadow" - a relentless tracker that targets your exact location and chases with high aggression.
  • Pinky (Pink): The "Ambusher" - constantly attempts to get ahead of you, targeting the space just in front of your movement path.
  • Inky (Cyan):The "Capricious" - possesses a bizarre AI that merges your position with Blinky's, acting erratically based on both to ambush from unexpected angles.
  • Clyde (Orange): The "Pokey" - behaves aggressively until getting close, then cowardly retreats back toward the lower-left corner.

Bonus Rations: Fruit

  • Appearance: Transient fruit items appear momentarily in the center of the maze to boost scores.
  • Recommendation:Prioritize these items whenever safe, as they significantly bolster high-score efforts without increasing risk factors.
